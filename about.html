My Story

I've spent over 7 years building a successful career in sales, consistently ranking among the top performers and earning recognition including President's Club Winner 2024 and Elite Performer status. But I realized early on that the future of business wasn't just about working harder—it was about working smarter.

That's what drove me to pursue my MBA in Artificial Intelligence and Strategic Management at Villanova University. I wanted to understand how AI could transform the way businesses operate, how sales teams could leverage technology to build better relationships, and how companies could automate repetitive tasks to focus on what truly matters: growth and innovation.

What sets me apart is that I don't just understand the technology—I understand business. I've been in the trenches, making cold calls, building customer relationships, exceeding quotas, and solving real operational challenges. I know what it takes to drive results because I've done it myself, year after year.

The Unique Perspective I Bring

Most AI consultants come from purely technical backgrounds. They understand algorithms and models, but they don't always understand the customer journey, the sales process, or the day-to-day operational challenges businesses face.

I bridge that gap. I speak both languages—business and technology. I can translate complex AI concepts into practical solutions that actually move the needle for your organization.

What I Do

I help businesses leverage AI without the complexity. Whether you're looking to optimize your sales operations, automate time-consuming processes, or explore new AI opportunities, I provide hands-on support from strategy to execution.

I create personalized AI chatbots tailored to your business needs, develop training videos to help your team adopt new technologies, and work alongside you to implement solutions that deliver real, measurable ROI.

My Mission

Helping businesses leverage AI without the complexity. Making cutting-edge technology accessible, practical, and profitable for companies of all sizes.
